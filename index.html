FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work?▼
You get 14 days of full access to Servio on your chosen plan — completely free. No credit card is required to start. At the end of your trial, you can choose to subscribe or cancel with zero obligation.
Comment fonctionne l'essai gratuit ?▼
Vous bénéficiez de 14 jours d'accès complet à Servio sur le plan de votre choix — entièrement gratuit. Aucune carte bancaire n'est requise pour commencer. À la fin de votre essai, vous pouvez choisir de vous abonner ou d'annuler sans aucune obligation.
Is my restaurant data secure?▼
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We never sell or share your data with third parties. Servio is fully compliant with GDPR and PIPEDA regulations. You can request deletion of your data at any time.
Can I cancel anytime?▼
Yes — cancel anytime from your account settings with one click. You'll keep full access until the end of your current billing period. There are no cancellation fees, no hidden charges, and no long-term contracts.
Does the bilingual feature really switch everything?▼
Yes. Toggle between FR and EN using the button in the app header. All interface labels and all AI-generated content — review replies, social posts, email campaigns — switch instantly to the selected language. Perfect for Montréal and other bilingual markets.
How is Servio different from generic AI tools?▼
Servio is built specifically for restaurants. Every prompt, every template, and every output is optimized for the restaurant industry. Generic AI tools require you to explain context every time. Servio already knows your cuisine type, city, and vibe — and generates content that actually sounds like a real restaurant, not a chatbot.

❓ Questions fréquentes
Comment configurer mon restaurant ?▼
Renseignez le nom, le type de cuisine, la ville et l'ambiance dans la barre en haut de chaque page. Ces informations permettent à Servio de personnaliser tout votre contenu automatiquement.
Que se passe-t-il si j'atteins ma limite mensuelle ?▼
Une bannière d'avertissement s'affiche lorsque vous atteignez votre limite. Vous pouvez upgrader votre plan à tout moment pour continuer sans interruption.
How does the FR/EN toggle work?▼
Click FR or EN in the top right corner of the app. The entire interface and all AI-generated content will switch languages instantly. Your restaurant profile stays the same.
Comment annuler mon abonnement ?▼
Rendez-vous dans Mon compte → Abonnement → Annuler. Vous conservez l'accès jusqu'à la fin de votre période de facturation. Aucun frais d'annulation.
